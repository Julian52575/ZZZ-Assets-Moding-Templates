--- INFO ---

WHAT YOU HAVE
- .dds = Game Texture (many posters into a single texture)
- .xcf = File to WORK on the texture

WHAT YOU NEED
- Gimp = FREE TOOL (https://www.gimp.org/downloads/)

WHY ITS GOOD
- Many texture available in the pack
- Each texture are cut into "layers" for easy manipulation
- Cool drawings to easily recognize where the poster is
- Arrows to recognize where the top is
- Free (No $)

--- STEPS ---

1. Open a .xcf
2. Select a picture layer (bottom right) (https://www.digitalphotomentor.com/how-to-use-layers-gimp/)
3. Draw or import your png/jpg into the layer
4. SAVE your progress into the .xcf 
5. EXPORT your image into the .dds texture (make sure to INCLUDE ALL LAYERS)
